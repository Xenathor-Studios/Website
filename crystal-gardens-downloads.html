Instructions (Windows):

1.) Download version of choice above.
2.) Open up C:\Users\USERNAME\AppData\Roaming\.minecraft (or type " %appdata% " into Run) *NOTE*: The /AppData folder may be hidden. To show, follow the instructions here.
3.) Open .minecraft\mods
4.) Move Crystal Gardens Mod-v.*.*.*-mc*.*.*.jar into the \mods folder.
5.) *NOTE*: Make sure Forge is correctly installed. For information regarding downloading Forge, read parts 1 and 2 of this WikiHow. Remember to download Minecraft Forge for 1.7.10!
