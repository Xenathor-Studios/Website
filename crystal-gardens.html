Crystal Gardens

Crystal Gardens is a Minecraft mod for MC version 1.7.10 (for now, I may or may not make it for more recent versions). It adds a variety of new minerals to enhance your mining experience! Introducing Cinnabar, Garnet, Ruby, Sapphire, Serpentine, Topaz, and new usages for Diamond, Gold, and Obsidian! Make new blocks and tools to create amazing structures!

Downloads page with instructions is here.

MODPACK-MAKERS: Please feel free to include this in your modpack if you want! However, I only ask that you include a link to this site (xenathorstudios.com) on your permissions page.
