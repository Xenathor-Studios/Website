The Warp Project

The Warp Project is a space/action 2D sidescroller created by Xenathor Studios and written in Java. The Interstellar Warp Drive has been completed; you are selected as the pilot of the ship that will extend humanity's reach beyond our system. What lies out there? Is there life? Or is space inhabited solely by celestial bodies? Play to find out!
